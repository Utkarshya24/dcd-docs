## Features

#### **Blazing-Fast Bare Metal Performance**

Say goodbye to noisy neighbors and resource contention. Your apps run on dedicated, high-performance bare metal servers for optimal speed and reliability.

#### **Effortless Scalability**

Scale your applications up or down in seconds, automatically, with our intelligent scaling features. Handle traffic spikes with ease and only pay for the resources you actually use. Plus, with our scale-to-zero capability, you'll never pay for idle servers again.

#### **Secure & Isolated Deployments**

Your applications are deployed in their own isolated microvms, with private networking and robust encryption to keep your data safe.

#### **Developer-Friendly Dashboard**

Manage everything from deployments and scaling to monitoring and billing through our intuitive, user-friendly dashboard. It's designed to be easy to use, even for small teams without dedicated DevOps resources.

#### **Transparent, Pay-As-You-Go Pricing**

No hidden fees, no surprises. Our pay-per-minute billing ensures you only pay for the compute time you actually use.
